Engineering team

Advent Steel & Engineering Ltd. was formed by a group of top steel building professionals who were focused on delivering the best products and services possible. It's no secret in this industry: You're only as good as your steel building experts and their skill in engineering and detailing. And in those areas, Advent Steel & Engineering Ltd. is second to none! State of the art technology and first class engineering enable us to serve as metal building experts who build a custom structure as easily and economically as a standard box building. Using only the finest materials available to the metal buildings industry we are proud to say: "We are the company that other companies use to set their standards."

All of our parts are manufactured to the highest tolerances according to the Metal Building Manufacturers Association. From the welds on our I-Beams to our long lasting paint finishes, we only allow the best parts to leave the factory doors because we know that our buildings represent our company. This level of quality is only possible because of the depth of experience present in our team of pre-engineered steel building experts. At Advent Steel & Engineering Ltd. Metal Buildings, our team knows how to deliver the best of the best in steel structures.

At Advent Steel & Engineering Ltd. we don't just sell the buildings, we also design, engineer, and detail our own buildings - with all services performed and delivered by our steel structure professionals. You can be sure that your order is in good hands every step of the way: from your initial quote to the day of delivery. Our team of prefabricated steel structure professionals are experienced in all types of buildings - from airplane hangars to multi story structures. Even if your building is a small backyard shop, you can be sure that we will handle your project with the same amount of attention to detail and professionalism that we provide all of our customers.

About Us

Advent Steel & Engineering Limited was established in 2015. It has owned pre-engineered steel buildings manufacturing plant that spans over 25,000 square feet on the outskirts of Dhaka, at Keraniganj (approximately 15 Kilometers from Baitul Mukarram Mosque, Dhaka).
